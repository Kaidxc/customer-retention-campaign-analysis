# Business Brief

## Project title

Customer Retention and Campaign Targeting Analysis for a UK Online Retailer

## Business stakeholder

CRM Marketing Manager

## Business problem

The retailer has a limited campaign budget and cannot contact every inactive customer.

The marketing team needs to identify high-value customers who appear at risk of becoming inactive.

## Business decision

Which customers should be prioritised for a retention campaign?

## Project objective

Use historical transaction data to:

1. measure customer purchasing behaviour;
2. identify valuable customers at risk of inactivity;
3. create a prioritised campaign target list;
4. propose how an A/B test could measure campaign impact.

## Primary KPIs

- Repeat purchase rate
- Average order value
- Revenue per customer
- Days since last purchase
- Customer retention rate
- Number of target customers
